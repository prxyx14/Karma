*--Importance Of Understanding About Karma--*


KARMA is a Brahmanic idea developed by the Buddhists. Some believe that karma is moral cause and effect.
Basically karma is the idea of "what goes around comes around", whether it is negative or positive.
Buddhism and Hinduism feel that karma determines the nature of a person's next existence.

Why KARMA...??


☝From its Sanskrit origin, Karma primarily means “action.” Karma is an eastern philosophy that emphasizes that individual actions determine the future consequences of the individual- “what you give in life, you will get in return. ”
